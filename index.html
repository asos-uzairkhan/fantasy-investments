📜 Competition Rules

  • Investment Options (at least 1 of each): Funds (no more than 1), stocks/shares, commodities, and cryptocurrencies
  • Starting Capital: Each participant starts with £50 virtual money
  • Equal Distribution: Your £50 is split equally across all active investments for the day
  • Substitute Investments: Every month, you can substitute one investment for another with substitutions taking effect at the start of the next month
  • Participation Prize: £5 for beating the S&P 500
  • Winner Prize: Profit you made (e.g. Winning value of £67 will earn you a prize of £17)
  • Duration: Start of January (01/01/2026) - End of December (31/12/2026)
